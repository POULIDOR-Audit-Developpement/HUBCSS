# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in this project should remain respectful, technical, and focused
on improving the work. Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language,
and disruptive behavior are not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- Assume good intent while staying precise about technical concerns.
- Critique code and decisions, not people.
- Keep reviews and discussions factual, concise, and actionable.
- Respect different levels of experience and different use cases.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or intimidation.
- Insults, derogatory language, or discriminatory remarks.
- Repeated off-topic or intentionally disruptive communication.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove contributions that violate this
code of conduct and may restrict participation when needed.
